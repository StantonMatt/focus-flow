# Focus Flow - Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 2026

## Overview

Focus Flow is a productivity Chrome extension that helps users stay focused by blocking distracting websites, tracking browsing time, and providing a Pomodoro timer. **We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

## Data Collection

**Focus Flow does NOT collect any personal data.** 

All information is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API:
- Blocked sites list and preferences
- Scheduling settings
- Pomodoro timer settings
- Time tracking statistics (domains visited and time spent)
- Friction mode settings

## Data Storage

- ✅ All data remains on your device
- ✅ No data is sent to external servers
- ✅ No analytics or tracking services are used
- ✅ No accounts or sign-ups required
- ✅ No cookies are used
- ✅ Data is never sold or shared with third parties

## Permissions Explained

The extension requires certain browser permissions to function:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| **storage** | Save your preferences and time tracking data locally |
| **alarms** | Run the Pomodoro timer in the background |
| **notifications** | Alert you when timer sessions end |
| **host_permissions** | Apply site blocking and time tracking on websites |

## Third-Party Services

Focus Flow does not use any third-party services, analytics, or external APIs. The extension functions entirely offline and locally.

## Data Deletion

All extension data can be deleted by:
1. Removing the extension from Chrome, or
2. Clearing the extension's storage via Chrome settings

## Children's Privacy

Focus Flow does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.
